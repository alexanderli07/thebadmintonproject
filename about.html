Who We Are

The people behindthe Project.

The Badminton Project exists to provide our community with genuine high-performance and competitive badminton training — built by people who take the game seriously.

What We Stand For

Three things we hold to.

  • Excellence

    High-performance standards in everything we do — from elite athlete development to a casual games night.

  • Development

    Athletes are built, not found. Every program is one step in a longer journey, and we take every step seriously.

  • Community

    Badminton is the game; people are the point. Social nights matter here as much as podiums.
