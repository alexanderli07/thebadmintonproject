Who We Are

Every great athlete
begins as a project.

The Badminton Project exists to provide our community with genuine high-performance and competitive badminton training — and a place where love of the game is taken seriously.

We build athletes deliberately: high-performance competitors pursuing the top of the sport, developing players finding their competitive game, and adults who want more from their badminton than a casual rally.

Development here is earned step by step — with intention, with standards, and with a community behind you.

What We Do

Training with intention.

Two training streams and a full calendar of adult badminton — each built with the same care, whether you are chasing titles or a great night on court.

01

Athletic Development Programs

High-Performance Training

For the committed competitive athlete. Structured, high-intensity training designed to build complete players — technically, tactically, physically — and carry them toward the highest levels of the sport.

02

Intro & Development Programs

Competitive Training

Where the competitive journey begins. Programs for new and developing competitors that build sound technique, movement and match craft — step by step, in an environment that takes their goals seriously.

03

Training · Games Nights · Social Nights

Adult Programs

Badminton for life. Adult training programs to sharpen your game, alongside games nights and badminton social nights — because the game belongs to everyone, at every stage.
