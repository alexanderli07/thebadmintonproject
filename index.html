Who We Are

Every great athlete
begins as a project.

The Badminton Project exists to provide our community with genuine high-performance and competitive badminton training — and a place where love of the game is taken seriously.

We build athletes deliberately: high-performance competitors pursuing the top of the sport, developing players finding their competitive game, and adults who want more from their badminton than a casual rally.

Development here is earned step by step — with intention, with standards, and with a community behind you.
