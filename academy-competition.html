The Program

What to expect.

Who it’s for

Young athletes ready to represent the Project in tournaments — players who want a team standard to hold to, on and off the court.

What they’ll train

Tournament preparation, match simulation, doubles chemistry and rotation, pre-competition routines, and match review. Your child trains alongside teammates who push them and hold the same standard.

What your child walks away with

Match-ready sharpness, a team that has their back, and the confidence that comes from competing as part of something bigger than themselves.
